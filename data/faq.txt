Q: How can I access my payslip?
A: Payslips are available on the company HR portal under 'Documents'.

Q: Where can I find the employee handbook?
A: The employee handbook is located in the 'Documents' section of the HR portal.

Q: What is the process for applying for leave?
A: Submit your leave request through the HR portal. Approval will be notified by email.

Q: How do I update my personal information?
A: You can update your personal information in the HR portal under 'Profile Settings'.

Q: Who do I contact for IT support?
A: Please contact the IT helpdesk at it-support@company.com or via the IT ticketing system.

Q: How can I request company documents?
A: Company documents can be requested from HR or downloaded from the HR portal.

Q: What are the company’s remote work policies?
A: Remote work is allowed with manager approval. Refer to the HR portal for guidelines.
